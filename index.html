About Us

We are a dedicated team of people reuniting lost property with its rightful owner.

Our team will do everything possible to reunite precious items that have been left behind whilst dealing with other important things passing through the Airport. Our dedicated team insures that all care is taken to secure, protect and reunite your items in timely manner and in safe hands. If you think you have left something onboard your flight or at one of Airport lounges, please contact us and we will search our database to find your item.
