Terms

We promise this is all the fine print.

We keep items for a maximum of three months from the date of loss. Once we have confirmed that we have found and catalogued your property, it may be collected from our offices near London Heathrow Airport. Please note that items received from the Airlines and Lounges may not be immediately available for collection, so check before collection. We can also send your items by courier at additional cost. We charge a fee to help cover the cost of collecting and storing your property and will require valid identification before we release goods for collection or delivery. If you would like someone else to collect the property on your behalf, you will need to provide a signed letter or email of authorization for them to do so. We will reconfirm these details for you when validating your claim. Items not matched to an enquiry or claimed within three months from the date of loss become the property of We Find It LTD. We will remove and securely destroy any personal data before either donating the items to charities, or recycling, disposing of, or selling them. Any revenue generated from unclaimed items contributes towards the cost of running the We Find It LTD.
